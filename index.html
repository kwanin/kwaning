Camping Checklist

  • A tent is essential for camping. Choose a tent that suits your needs and the size of your group.
  • A good sleeping bag will keep you warm and comfortable at night. Choose a sleeping bag that is appropriate for the weather conditions.
  • A ground pad will provide insulation and cushioning for your sleeping bag. Choose a ground pad that is appropriate for your sleeping bag and the terrain.
